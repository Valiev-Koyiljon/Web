# ESS를 활용한 에너지 비용 및 판매 가격 계산 방법 및 장치

**Inventor:** Koyiljon Valiev  
**Organization:** Recs Innovation Ltd, Naju, South Korea  
**Issued by:** Korean Intellectual Property Office (KIPO)  
**Country:** South Korea  
**Status:** Issued

## Summary

This patent was invented by Koyiljon Valiev during his tenure at Recs Innovation Ltd in South Korea. It describes a method and apparatus for calculating energy costs and sales prices using Energy Storage Systems (ESS). The invention enables intelligent pricing decisions in smart grid environments by factoring in real-time ESS state, grid market conditions, and energy arbitrage opportunities.
