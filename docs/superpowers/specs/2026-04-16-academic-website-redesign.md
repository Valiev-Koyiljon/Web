# Academic Portfolio Website Redesign — Design Spec

**Date:** 2026-04-16
**Status:** Approved

## Goal

Replace dark glassmorphism portfolio with a clean, white, academic-style personal website inspired by top AI researchers (Karpathy, LeCun, Fei-Fei Li). Substance over style.

## Design Decisions

### Color Palette
- Background: #FFFFFF
- Text primary: #1a1a2e
- Text secondary: #4a5568
- Text muted: #718096
- Accent: #2563EB (professional blue)
- Accent hover: #1d4ed8
- Borders: #e5e7eb
- Light bg sections: #f8fafc

### Typography
- Font: Inter (system fallback), JetBrains Mono for code/tech
- Generous whitespace, 60-80 char line length
- No flashy animations

### Sections
1. Hero: photo + name + title + bio + CTA + social
2. News & Updates
3. Work Experience (timeline)
4. Research & Publications
5. Patents
6. Skills (categorized)
7. Education
8. Achievements & Awards
9. Courses
10. Contact
11. Footer
